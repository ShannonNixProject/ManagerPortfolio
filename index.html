Challenge: The company relied on Workamajig for project management and client tracking, but the platform lacked flexibility, had a steep learning curve, and did not fully meet the team’s needs. Transitioning to ClickUp for project management and HubSpot for CRM and sales processes was necessary, but without integration, the team faced siloed data, redundant manual entry, workflow misalignment, and limited visibility into project progress—leading to inefficiencies, communication gaps, and delays.

Solution (3-month Agile migration plan):

  • Developed a phased migration strategy to transfer all relevant data from Workamajig without disrupting operations.
  • Automated client interactions using HubSpot workflows, reducing manual data entry and improving communication.
  • Centralized task management and project tracking in ClickUp to enhance visibility and streamline collaboration.
  • Integrated HubSpot and ClickUp using API connections and Zapier for real-time data synchronization.
  • Established standardized workflows to align sales and project teams, ensuring a smooth transition.
  • Conducted iterative testing and sprint reviews to refine integrations before full deployment.

Impact:

  • ✔ 50% reduction in manual data entry, increasing efficiency and accuracy.

Optimizing Payroll Efficiency: Implementing a Scalable Solution for Multi-Entity Operations

Challenge: Managing payroll across 14 separate entities, each with unique structures, policies, and compliance requirements, was complex and time-consuming. The outdated payroll system caused delays, compliance risks, and errors, increasing administrative burden. The lack of integration with other business functions further complicated tracking and reconciliation across entities.

Solution (6-month Waterfall implementation):

  • Conducted a needs assessment to identify critical pain points and requirements.
  • Researched and selected a payroll platform with automation, multi-entity capabilities, and seamless integration with accounting and HR systems.
  • Developed standardized payroll processes across all entities to improve efficiency and reduce errors.
  • Provided training and documentation for payroll teams to ensure a smooth transition and adoption of the new system.
  • Ran parallel payroll cycles for 2 months to validate accuracy before full implementation.
  • Executed a phased deployment to minimize risks and ensure a seamless transition.

Impact:

  • ✔ 40% reduction in payroll processing time due to automation.
  • ✔ Improved payroll accuracy, significantly reducing errors and compliance risks.
  • ✔ Standardized payroll procedures, increasing efficiency across 14 entities.

Ensuring Data Accuracy and Efficiency in Device ID Marketing

Challenge: The company implemented a Device ID marketing program to enhance targeted advertising and customer engagement. However, incorrect data from a third-party agency and internal record-keeping inconsistencies led to misallocated ad spend, ineffective targeting, and unreliable campaign tracking. Discrepancies between agency reports and internal records undermined trust in campaign data, making it difficult to measure ROI and optimize future marketing efforts.

Solution (4-month Lean Six Sigma approach):

  • Performed a root cause analysis (DMAIC method) to identify patterns of inaccuracies in third-party agency data.
  • Established a standardized data validation process before campaign launches to ensure only verified information was used.
  • Implemented an automated reconciliation system to compare agency data with internal tracking in real time, flagging inconsistencies for review.
  • Enhanced internal record-keeping practices to improve data consistency across marketing and sales teams.
  • Strengthened vendor accountability, setting clear expectations for data accuracy and implementing review protocols for discrepancies.
  • Adopted an iterative review process, using Lean Six Sigma methodologies to continually refine data accuracy controls.

Impact:

  • ✔ 30% improvement in campaign targeting, leading to higher engagement rates.
  • ✔ Significant reduction in data discrepancies, increasing confidence in marketing analytics.
  • ✔ More effective budget allocation, reducing wasted ad spend.
  • ✔ Streamlined reporting and decision-making, allowing precise adjustments to marketing strategies.
  • ✔ Stronger vendor accountability, ensuring long-term data accuracy and reliability.
